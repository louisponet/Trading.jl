OrderBook

CurrentModule = Trading

An OrderBook is a representation of the currently known Ask and Bid levels. Essentially, these are the quantities of a given Asset that people want to sell (Ask) or buy (Bid) at certain prices.

In Trading.jl they are implemented in the AssetLedger as 2 TreeComponents. These are standard Components that are also internally backed by a Red-Black Tree (facilitating easy search type operations).
